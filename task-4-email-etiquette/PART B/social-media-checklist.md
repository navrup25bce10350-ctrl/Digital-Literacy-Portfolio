# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do’s
Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and interactions.

Think carefully before posting any content online.

Use privacy settings to control who can view your information.

Share accurate and verified information only.

Build a positive digital footprint by sharing achievements and useful content.



## ❌ Don’ts
Do not share sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, addresses, or passwords.

Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.

Do not click on suspicious links or respond to unknown messages.

Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.

Do not overshare personal details that could affect your reputation.

#  Prevention Checklist: 

## Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist
-Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone.

-Do not approve “collect requests” unless you are absolutely sure of the sender.

-Always verify the identity of the person before making any payment.

-Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.

-Use only official and trusted payment applications.

-Enable transaction alerts and regularly monitor your bank statements.

-Be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true.

-Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.


## UPI / Financial Safety Tips
-Always double-check the receiver’s name before confirming a payment.

-Remember that receiving money does not require entering a UPI PIN.
